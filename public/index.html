Catch my projects

Projects in Web Development are fast in different ways.. and so are mine!

To catch them - I've got to admit they're kind of jumpy - you have to drop the e-magnet on top of them and activate the power. Once catched, get them to the slot to mount them there and unlock the project details!

use the ARROW KEYS to control the magnet and

press the SPACE BAR (and keep it pressed) until you reached the slot

once the project is mounted you can look at the details or eject it..

have fun!
